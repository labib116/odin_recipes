Salad

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Red onion
  • Bell peppers
  • Avocado
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Feta cheese
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.)
  • Dried cranberries or raisins

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cucumber, tomatoes, carrots, red onion, and bell peppers.
  3. Add avocado slices on top.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss gently to combine.
  7. Add feta cheese, nuts, and dried cranberries or raisins if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
